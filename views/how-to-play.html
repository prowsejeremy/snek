How to Play

Seriously? You really need the instructions? It's snake... Ok, no judgement, (maybe a little judgement). Swipe on the map the direction you want the snake to move. You can go through the walls on this version. You'll see a few different pieces on the map as you play so here's a key to help you out:

Apples

You'll see these the most on the map, you want to collect these to score points and progress the game. Each one is worth 5 points.

Covid Spor

These prickly fellas will punish you by taking away 3 points if you collect them. So maintain social distancing and they'll despawn after 5 seconds.

Magic Treats

If you see these guys pop up you'll want to act quick. You'll have 5 seconds to catch them and they're worth 10 pts each!
